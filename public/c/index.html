Future Vision: In the future, when Apple and Google integrate OCR-to-hash verification into their camera apps, tapping a verify: link will automatically verify the claim made in your camera's viewfinder—instantly showing ✅ VERIFIED or ❌ FAILS VERIFICATION.

What is this?

This is a proof-of-concept for OCR-to-hash verification—a privacy-preserving way to verify printed claims (receipts, certificates, licenses, etc.) using only your phone's camera.

How it works:

  • Point your camera at a document with a verify: URL
  • OCR extracts the text entirely on-device (privacy-first)
  • Computes a SHA-256 hash of the normalized text
  • Checks if the issuer confirms that hash as authentic

Why it matters:

  • ✅ Images never leave your phone (100% client-side OCR)
  • ✅ No uploading documents to verification portals
  • ✅ Works for degrees, receipts, licenses, certificates
  • ✅ No blockchain, no QR codes cluttering documents
