Normalization rules applied:
  • Smart quotes: Curly apostrophe (’) → straight (')
  • Whitespace: Multiple spaces collapsed to one
  • Line endings: Standardized across platforms
  • Empty lines: Removed
  • Trim: Leading/trailing whitespace removed per line
Why this matters: OCR engines may produce slightly different characters depending on lighting, angle, or font rendering. Normalization ensures the same badge always produces the same hash, regardless of scanning conditions.
