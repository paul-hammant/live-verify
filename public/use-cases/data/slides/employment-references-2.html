Normalization rules applied:
  • Unicode dashes: En-dash (–) and em-dash (—) → hyphen (-)
  • multiple concurrent spaces: swapped for just one
  • Smart quotes: curly (“ ”)→ straight (")
  • Whitespace: Trim whitespace from start and end of lines
  • Empty lines: Removed
  • Non-breaking spaces: Converted to regular spaces
Why this matters: The same claim copied from Word, Gmail, or a PDF might have different invisible characters. Normalization ensures they all hash to the same value.
