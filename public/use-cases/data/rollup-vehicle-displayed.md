# Vehicle-Displayed Permits

Permits and licenses displayed in or on vehicles, visible through windshields or on dashboards. Passengers and enforcement can verify at a glance.

## Included Permit Types

- [Taxi/For-Hire Licenses](view.html?doc=taxi-for-hire-licenses) - Driver credentials displayed on dashboard
- [Taxi Driver Verification Cards](view.html?doc=taxi-driver-verification-cards) - Photo ID with license number
- [Vehicle Registrations](view.html?doc=vehicle-registrations) - Registration stickers/cards
- [Vehicle Inspection Reports](view.html?doc=vehicle-inspection-reports) - Safety inspection stickers
- [Commercial Vehicle Permits](view.html?doc=commercial-auto-insurance) - Interstate commerce
- [Parking Permits](view.html?doc=parking-receipts-validation) - Residential/business parking
- [Delivery Driver Verification](view.html?doc=delivery-driver-verification) - Gig economy credentials

## Common Display Requirements

- Visible through front windshield
- Dashboard-mounted or hanging from mirror
- Photo ID for driver-specific permits
- Expiration date clearly visible
- Often includes QR code for quick lookup

## Verification Value

**Problem:** Fake vehicle permits:
- Unlicensed taxis/rideshares with fake credentials
- Forged inspection stickers on unsafe vehicles
- Fake parking permits to avoid tickets
- Counterfeit commercial vehicle registrations

**Solution:** Passengers can verify taxi driver credentials before entering. Parking enforcement can verify permits are genuine. DOT inspectors can verify commercial vehicle compliance.
