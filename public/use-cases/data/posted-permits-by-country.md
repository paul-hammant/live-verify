# Posted Permits & Certificates by Country

Legal requirements for displaying permits and certificates vary significantly by jurisdiction. This file covers country-specific requirements for physically posted documents — the things that must be on the wall, in the window, or at the construction site.

The verification value is consistent: proving the displayed document matches what the issuing authority actually issued, preventing businesses from displaying outdated, revoked, or fabricated permits.

---

## France (Affichages Obligatoires)

French display requirements are notably strict, especially for construction permits where proof of continuous display affects legal appeal deadlines.

### Construction Permits (Permis de Construire)

French construction permits have some of the strictest display requirements in Europe.

**Panel Requirements (Article R*424-15 Code de l'urbanisme):**
- **Size:** Minimum 120cm × 80cm
- **Timing:** Within 8 days of permit issuance
- **Location:** Visible from public road, on the construction site
- **Duration:** Minimum 2 months continuous display

**Mandatory Information:**
- Beneficiary name/company
- Permit number and date
- Authorized floor area (surface de plancher)
- Building height in meters
- Nature of the project
- Address for file consultation
- Third-party appeal rights statement

**Appeal Rights Statement Required:**
> "Le délai de recours contentieux est de deux mois à compter du premier jour d'une période continue de deux mois d'affichage sur le terrain du présent panneau"

**Proof of Display:**
- Commissaire de justice (bailiff) visits recommended
- Three visits over 60 days to prove continuous display
- Without proof, appeals can be filed up to 6 months after completion

### Workplace Postings (Affichages Obligatoires en Entreprise)

**Required Postings:**
- **Convention collective** — Applicable collective agreement
- **Règlement intérieur** — Internal rules (companies with 50+ employees)
- **Horaires de travail** — Working hours
- **Repos hebdomadaire** — Weekly rest days
- **Consignes de sécurité** — Safety instructions
- **Interdiction de fumer** — No smoking signs
- **Coordonnées inspection du travail** — Labor inspectorate contact
- **Médecine du travail** — Occupational health service contact
- **Égalité professionnelle** — Equal opportunity statements
- **Lutte contre le harcèlement** — Anti-harassment notices

### Food & Hospitality

- **Licence de débit de boissons** — Alcohol license (displayed categories I-IV)
- **Affichage des prix** — Price display requirements
- **Origine des viandes** — Meat origin labeling
- **Allergènes** — Allergen information

### Vehicle Displays

- **Contrôle technique** — Vehicle inspection sticker (windshield)
- **Carte grise** — Registration document
- **Assurance** — Insurance certificate
- **Licence taxi/VTC** — Taxi and ride-share licenses

**Sources:**
- [Service-Public.fr - Affichage de l'autorisation d'urbanisme](https://www.service-public.gouv.fr/particuliers/vosdroits/F1988)
- [FFB - Réglementation affichage permis de construire](https://www.ffbatiment.fr/gestion-entreprise/organiser-mon-chantier/autorisations-regles-durbanisme/dossier/reglementation-affichage-panneau-permis-construire)

## Germany (Aushangpflichtige Gesetze)

German requirements focus heavily on workplace law postings, with a specific legal term for required displays.

### Workplace Postings (Aushangpflichtige Gesetze)

German employers must display extracts from various laws. The term "Aushangpflichtige Gesetze" refers to legislation required to be posted in the workplace.

**Key Required Postings:**
- **Arbeitsschutzgesetz (ArbSchG)** — Occupational Safety and Health Act
- **Arbeitszeitgesetz (ArbZG)** — Working Hours Act
- **Bundesurlaubsgesetz (BUrlG)** — Federal Vacation Act
- **Mutterschutzgesetz (MuSchG)** — Maternity Protection Act
- **Jugendarbeitsschutzgesetz (JArbSchG)** — Youth Employment Protection Act
- **Allgemeines Gleichbehandlungsgesetz (AGG)** — General Equal Treatment Act

**Display Requirements:**
- Must be accessible to all employees
- Can now be displayed digitally (accessible to remote workers)
- Many companies subscribe to services providing updated compilations
- Available free at gesetze-im-internet.de

### Construction Permits (Baugenehmigung)

**Bauschild (Construction Sign):**
- Must display building permit number
- Project description and scope
- Owner/developer information
- Architect and construction company
- Expected completion date

### Food & Hygiene

- HACCP compliance documentation
- Health department inspection results
- Staff hygiene training certificates

### Vehicle & Transport

- **TÜV stickers** — Vehicle inspection (on license plates)
- **Umweltplakette** — Environmental zone stickers
- **Taxi Konzession** — Taxi operating licenses
- **Güterkraftverkehr** — Commercial transport permits

### Licensed Premises

- **Gaststättenkonzession** — Restaurant/bar operating license
- **Gewerbeerlaubnis** — Business permit
- Opening hours (Öffnungszeiten) — where regulated

**Sources:**
- [Gesetze im Internet](https://www.gesetze-im-internet.de/)
- [Sawayo - Aushangpflichtige Gesetze Guide](https://en.sawayo.de/magazin/aushangpflichtige-gesetze)

## United Kingdom

UK requirements vary between England, Wales, Scotland, and Northern Ireland. Wales notably has stricter food hygiene display requirements.

### Food Hygiene Ratings (0-5 Scale)

The Food Hygiene Rating Scheme gives businesses a rating from 5 (very good) to 0 (urgent improvement necessary).

**Display Requirements:**
- **Wales & Northern Ireland:** Legally required to display at/near customer entrance
- **England:** Voluntary display (but encouraged)
- Must be visible before customers enter
- Green sticker with rating number

**Verification Value:** Customers can verify the displayed rating matches official records. Prevents businesses displaying old/fake stickers after a downgrade.

### Construction & Planning

**Planning Permission Notices:**
- Site notices must be posted for major applications
- Yellow notices on lamp posts for neighbors
- Display period typically 21 days

**Building Control Notices:**
- Completion certificates for building regulations
- Gas Safe certificates for boiler installations
- Electrical installation certificates (Part P)

### Workplace Postings

**Health & Safety Law Poster:**
- Required in all workplaces with employees
- Must display HSE-approved poster or provide equivalent leaflet
- Updated versions required when regulations change

**Employer's Liability Insurance Certificate:**
- Must be displayed where employees can easily read it
- Shows insurer name and policy number
- Digital display now permitted

### Licensed Premises

**Alcohol Licences:**
- Premises licence summary must be displayed
- Shows permitted hours and conditions
- Personal licence holder details

**Gambling Licences:**
- Betting shop and casino licences displayed
- Gaming machine permits visible

### Vehicle Displays

- **MOT certificates** — Vehicle roadworthiness
- **Tax discs** — Now digital, but historic requirement
- **Taxi/Private Hire licences** — Driver badge and vehicle plate
- **HGV operator discs** — Commercial vehicle compliance

**Sources:**
- [Food Standards Agency - Hygiene Rating Scheme](https://www.food.gov.uk/safety-hygiene/food-hygiene-rating-scheme)
- [FSA - Mandatory Display Requirements](https://www.food.gov.uk/research/mandatory-display)

## Verification Value

For all countries, the verification pattern is the same:

1. **Displayed permit** claims certain rights/status
2. **Verifier scans** the document
3. **Issuing authority confirms** the permit is current, not revoked, and matches what was issued

This catches:
- Outdated permits displayed after expiry
- Fabricated permits that were never issued
- Permits that have been revoked or suspended
- Altered permits (changed dates, expanded scope)

The domain binding is country-specific (e.g., `verify:service-public.gouv.fr`, `verify:food.gov.uk`) but the mechanism is universal.
