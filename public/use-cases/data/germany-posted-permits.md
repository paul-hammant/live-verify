# Germany Posted Permits & Certificates (Aushangpflichtige Gesetze)

Permits and legal notices required to be displayed in German workplaces and construction sites.

## Workplace Postings (Aushangpflichtige Gesetze)

German employers must display extracts from various laws. The term "Aushangpflichtige Gesetze" refers to legislation required to be posted in the workplace.

**Key Required Postings:**

- **Arbeitsschutzgesetz (ArbSchG)** - Occupational Safety and Health Act
- **Arbeitszeitgesetz (ArbZG)** - Working Hours Act
- **Bundesurlaubsgesetz (BUrlG)** - Federal Vacation Act
- **Mutterschutzgesetz (MuSchG)** - Maternity Protection Act
- **Jugendarbeitsschutzgesetz (JArbSchG)** - Youth Employment Protection Act
- **Allgemeines Gleichbehandlungsgesetz (AGG)** - General Equal Treatment Act

**Display Requirements:**
- Must be accessible to all employees
- Can now be displayed digitally (accessible to remote workers)
- Many companies subscribe to services providing updated compilations
- Available free at gesetze-im-internet.de

## Construction Permits (Baugenehmigung)

**Bauschild (Construction Sign):**
- Must display building permit number
- Project description and scope
- Owner/developer information
- Architect and construction company
- Expected completion date

## Food & Hygiene

**Hygiene certificates:**
- HACCP compliance documentation
- Health department inspection results
- Staff hygiene training certificates

## Vehicle & Transport

- **TÜV stickers** - Vehicle inspection (on license plates)
- **Umweltplakette** - Environmental zone stickers
- **Taxi Konzession** - Taxi operating licenses
- **Güterkraftverkehr** - Commercial transport permits

## Licensed Premises

- **Gaststättenkonzession** - Restaurant/bar operating license
- **Gewerbeerlaubnis** - Business permit
- Opening hours (Öffnungszeiten) - where regulated
